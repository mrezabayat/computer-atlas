Modern AI didn't appear from nowhere. It's the latest layer on top of decades of work on algorithms, statistics, neural networks, and accelerator hardware. This path takes you from "what's an algorithm" all the way to "what's a large language model", with just enough machine learning in between to make the last few topics make sense.

By the end you should be able to read a paper abstract, follow a technical blog post about LLMs, and know what questions to push back on when someone claims their AI product does something extraordinary.
